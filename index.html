The Dilemma

The Dilemma is a tragic interactive comic that follows the story of a university student who is torn over balancing academics, personal time, and relationships. In this light-hearted and humorous comic, users are given the interactive power to determine the fate of the main character through difficult choices between unfortunate situations.
